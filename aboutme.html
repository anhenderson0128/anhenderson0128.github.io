Alyssa lived in a small town in Connecticut until she was about the age of fourteen. During that time she developed a love for two things; animals and photography. You would often find Alyssa strolling through the woods taking pictures of birds and trees or at the stables taking pictures of horses and their riders. Alyssa's love for photography flourished when she got her first Nikon Camera for Christmas when she was thirteen. This same year is when Alyssa started horse back riding at a barn very close to her home. When she would have her lessons she would ask her parents to video-tape them and later she would edit them into little videos with music in the background. This quickly sparked Alyssa's passion for videography and editing.

Alyssa started her Freshman year of High School in a new state, New Hampshire. During the process of her family's move she looked for a school that had a great photography and videography program. Alyssa settled on Windham High School and soon entered into many videography classes. Alyssa took many classes during her Sophomore and Junior year and for her Senior year she did an internship with her teacher. Through Alyssa's high school years she realized her true passion is in the art of editing videos but her heart still has a love for photography. Now Alyssa is enrolled in the University of New Hampshire where she will graduate with a Communication Arts Degree with a specialization in Digital Media in the year 2022.
